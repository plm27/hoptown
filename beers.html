We believe that only the finest ingredients should go into making the finest beers. And that's our promise to you. Period.

We source our hops locally from Rockstar Rooftop Farms in Rockaway, Queens and our malts from the Hudson Valley. We not only believe in brewing organically for you, but also for the benefit of our planet. As an associate member of the American Organic Hop Grower Association, our locally grown hops reduce the carbon footprint of each bottle you bring home.

Our line of organic beers will suit every palate, you need only to crack open a bottle to find your new favorite.

Crystal Lake Lager: A true classic. Clean and crisp, with hints of sweet corn. Goes down easy.

Rogue IPA: A traditional IPA with a Queens twist. Hoppy and citrusy with floral and earth notes.

Porthill Porter: Our most robust brew featuring bold roasted malts and hints of exotic spices.
